Property Prices in Bromley and Croydon

  • Freehold Sales
  • Sold between 2021 - 2022


This Tableau visualisation was created for my individual final project during my Data Science Bootcamp at General Assembly. The dataset was generated by webscraping details of 300,000 properties from Zoopla.co.uk over the course of a week. The final property count in the visualisation is only 7,617 as these represent a subset of A) properties sold in 2021-2022 B) in Bromley and Croydon C) where the sale was freehold rather than for leasehold.

The dataset was limited in this way specifically because Zoopla does not provide any information about the length of lease when properties are sold leasehold. This adds a huge amount of uncertainty when predicting sale price which I decided to avoid altogether by only looking at freehold.
